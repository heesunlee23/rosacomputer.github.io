Heesun Lee (Rosa) is an external researcher at Computer Security Lab at Sookmyung Women's University, Seoul, South Korea, advised by Professor Younghoon Park.
She received a bachelor's degree in Engineering in Computer Science from Sookmyung Women's University (SMU) in 2018. As an external researcher, she is participating in "Development of Hashgraph-based Blockchain Enhancement Scheme and Implementation of Testbed for Autonomous Driving" project, granted by Institute of Information & Communication Technology & Evaluation
As of Fall 2023, tentatively, she starts to pursue a MS/PhD integrated degree at Department of Computer Science at SMU under the supervision of Professor Younghoon Park.
Her current research areas are software security, modern cryptography, and post-quantum cryptography.
As of today, I am not a member of Sookmyung Women's University and I am not a student, employee, or professor at Sookmyung Women's University.
