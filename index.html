Heesun Lee (Rosa) is an external researcher at Computer Security Lab at Sookmyung Women's University (SMWU), Seoul, South Korea, under the supervision of Professor Younghoon Park.
She received a bachelor's degree in Engineering in Computer Science from Sookmyung Women's University (SMU) in 2018.
As an external researcher, she is participating in "Development of Hashgraph-based Blockchain Enhancement Scheme and Implementation of Testbed for Autonomous Driving" project, granted by Institute of Information & Communication Technology & Evaluation.
As of Fall 2023, tentatively, she returns to Rochester, New York to finish her Computer Science master program in Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT).
She is interested in a wide range of fields such as networking, security, cryptography and its applications, embedded systems, databases, large data processing, software engineering, etc
As of today, I am not a member of SMWU.
